HAVE A CONVERSATION WITH GOD (II)

DAY 3: HAVE A CONVERSATION WITH GOD (II)

By Ruth Abioye

27 Dec, 2020

Core Bible Verses: Ex 19-20;


God is interested in communicating with you. In the Old Testament, the only way He could communicate with His people was through a Priest and the challenge with that is that the information may be miscommunicated.


The children of Israel one day requested to hear the voice of God and when God showed up and started speaking, there was so much majesty, awesomeness, and thundering in His voice that they trembled at every word He spoke – they then begged Moses (the chosen Priest) to hear God on their behalf and speak to them.


This is what the majority of us have done in our lives, we have made up our minds that it is impossible to have a relationship with God and so, therefore, have relegated that responsibility to men/women of God. The challenge with this is that it is not everyone that is genuinely hearing from God and because you don’t have a relationship with God, they are leading you astray without your knowledge.


When God spoke to the children of Israel, they trembled in fear but when God spoke to Moses, he trembled with Fear for most high God. The more you speak to God, the more you love and reverence Him – the fear the children of Israel experienced was the fear of unworthiness but the fear that Moses will feel in the presence of God was a fear of reverence – experiencing the Holy nature of God.


A conversation with God is getting to know God! It is not shouting your list of needs to God but it is speaking to Him and listening to hear what He would say to you.

ENEMIES TO BUILDING RELATIONSHIP WITH GOD

  • - Fear

  • - Unforgiveness/Resentment

  • - Comparison

  • - False prophecies and encounters

  • - Past mistakes: The enemy’s job is to ensure that you continuously feel unworthy of the mercy and grace of God. There are things you may have done which every time you try to speak to God or read your Bible, the enemy keeps bringing what you have done to your mind and tell you that you are not good enough.

God made the greatest sacrifice by sending Jesus Christ to die for us so that everyone can have a personal relationship with Him. The thing I love most about God is that He moves at your own pace. He doesn’t expect you to be perfect from the first day you get saved (and neither should you put such pressure on yourself). HE wants to walk with you daily and teach you the way to enjoy a profitable life without stress.


Stop waiting for someone to hear God on your behalf! Start building your relationship with Him.


Please note, you may not hear His voice audibly but He speaks through His word so allow the HolySpirit to teach you and guide you to hear the voice of God.


MAKE IT PERSONAL

Think about and write down the things that have stopped you from having a constant relationship with God?


PRAYER

Heavenly Father, I thank you for your unconditional love towards me and I thank you for continuously looking out for me. Dear Lord, I ask you to help me in building up a relationship with you. I repent if I have allowed the things easily beset me and take your place in my life. Knowing that you are faithful and Just to forgive when I repent, I believe I am forgiven and I walk in the newness of light. I receive the HolySpirit to help me and teach me the things that I don’t know about you. I thank you my Father and I love you.
