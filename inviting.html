INVITING HIM IN

DAY 1: INVITING HIM IN

By Ruth Abioye

25 Dec, 2020


Core Bible Verses: Song of Solomon 5:2; Jere 31:3; John 17:23


The names of God and their meanings teach us important lessons about the character and power of God. All the names of God show an attribute of who He is!!!


If your best friend is outside your door, no matter how much mutual love exists between both of you, both of you can't be together if you do not let the person in. If there is a persistent knock and there is no response, the person will leave after a while.


You cannot know the heart of God if you do not let Him into yours. You have to make a conscious effort to learn about who He is


You matter to God.


  • - He made you in His image,

  • - He breathe into you His Spirit so that you can have the God – Kind of Life

  • - He called you by His name. What you are going through, or your past does not change the name God gave to you

  • - You are loved by the Almighty

Our minds cannot grasp the love of God for us. Stop trying so hard to define the love of God with your human mind – your mind cannot grasp it.


Your life may not make sense to you and circumstances around you may want you to doubt the Love of God for you.


Just let God be God and rest in Him and then you will realize that all the devil was trying to do is to blind your eyes from the love and be clouded with darkness thereby causing depression, self-hatred, etc.


God has always wanted to be a part of your life that is why God is constantly knocking. He is just hoping one day, you will open the door. He has so much to tell you, so much to show you, so much to make you. For any of these to happen, you have to let Him in


MAKE IT PERSONAL

Write in your journal the names of God you know and what you have experienced of God through that name.


Declaration

I am valuable to God. I matter to God; I belong to God! God loves me as much as He loves Jesus. I destroy every thought that depresses me and keeps me in darkness. I receive the Love God has for me!!!
