STEPS TO KNOWING GOD

DAY 5: STEPS TO KNOWING GOD

By Ruth Abioye

25 Dec, 2020

God is not a faraway creature that just created the earth and pushed it afar. He is very much interested in your life and wants to be a part of your life.


The most interesting thing about God is that in all His ‘powerfulness’, He never forces Himself on us (He has goodwill concerning us but if we do not want that will, He cannot force it on us).


To have a relationship with God means that you have to be intentional just as He is towards you


Be Born again –

  • - You have to accept Him as the One you want to have a relationship with.

  • - You have to begin to relate to Him as One who you want to pour your love on.


Allow the Holy Spirit to gain access into your life


You cannot know God without Jesus and you cannot know Jesus without the Holy Spirit. The Holy Spirit has been mandated to show us the deep things of God – i.e. when we allow the Holy Spirit, He begins to reveal to us things that are in the heart of God. 1 Cor 2:9-10


Deep things are only for lovers.


Get rid of unrighteous lifestyle – friends, habits, ideologies, etc


Create time daily to spend time with God


  • - Set a time daily that is convenient for you – set an alarm

  • - Find a quiet place

  • - Get your journal, Bible, and writing materials

  • - Invite the Holy Spirit in

  • - Play some worship music or just worship God


MAKE IT PERSONAL

Prayer: Sweet Holy Spirit, I invite you into my life today. I open my heart to receive, I open my ears to hear and I open my eyes to all that you want to show me.
