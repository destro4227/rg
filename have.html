HAVE A CONVERSATION WITH GOD (I)

DAY 2: HAVE A CONVERSATION WITH GOD (I)

By Ruth Abioye


26 Dec, 2020

Core Bible Verses: Ex 33:11


A relationship can never work without proper communication. Stop thinking to yourself that God is too far away and there is no way to have a conversation with Him.


Time and Time again God showed us in scriptures that He is eager to hear from us and He wants to speak to us:


  • a. Come let us reason together – Isa 1:18

  • b. Call unto me and I will answer you – Jere 33:3

  • c. I will stand upon my watch and see what the Lord is saying – Hab 2:1

  • d. As far as your eyes can see. Gen 13:14-17

  • e. Remember how I have served you faithfully and with wholehearted devotion, and how I have carried out your will – II Kings 20:3; Isa 38:3

A conversation with God is getting to know God! It is not shouting your list of needs to God but it is speaking to Him and listening to hear what He would say to you.


To have a conversation with God, you need to


  • 1. Be born again

    • We hear this in church every Sunday and we have become so used to it that we probably don’t even get its essence anymore!


    If you are not sure of your salvation, then you are not saved! Maybe you have actually and genuinely been saved before but a lot happened and you haven’t spoken to God in a while or you just shut down your mind from receiving anything and everything that has to do with God.


  • 2. Know His voice. John 10:27

  • 3. Learn to be quiet.

  • 4. Pray in the Holy Spirit. Rom 8:26; I Cor 2:9-10; Jude 1:20

  • 5. Study. II Tim 2:15


MAKE IT PERSONAL


Write in your journal the names of God you know and what you have experienced of God through that name.



Begin Having a conversation with God today – you can write in your journal what is in your mind and how you want God to perceive all that is within you!
