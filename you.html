YOU ARE JUST LIKE GOD

DAY 4: YOU ARE JUST LIKE GOD

By Ruth Abioye

25 Dec, 2020

Core Scriptures: Gen 1:26-28; Ps 82:6; 2 Cor 5:17; 1 John 4:17


God made us in His image and likeness. This means we have His eyes, His mind, His mouth, His heart, etc.


Do not let anybody make you feel inferior – people’s idea of you is an opinion; God's idea of you is the truth.


This is why it is important to know God. If you don’t know God, then you cannot see your true identity and if you don’t find that then you can live the life God called you to live


Jesus was able to shut the mouth of everyone that came against Him because He had a relationship with the Father – He knew who God was to Him, He knew who He was in God and He knew what God had called Him to do so anything contrary to that was a lie from the pi pf hell and He was not going to accept it.


You have to be born again to come into your true identity. Life begins when you accept Jesus into your life. It is His breath that gives meaning to your existence. Acts 17:28


MAKE IT PERSONAL

Get your journal – write down 3-5 things you found about God (just like the example above)


Now meditate and declare these things to yourself all day till the word be planted in your heart.


Confession:

I am a child of God; I carry the DNA of my Father God; I am a duplicate of Him on earth. God is awesome – I am the image of awesomeness (Gen 1:26-28); God is wonderful – I am full of wonders; God is love – I am the object of God’s affection (Deut 10:15); God is caring – I am the apple of His eyes!
