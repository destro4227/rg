When you meet someone new, the first thing you ask is WHAT IS YOUR NAME?

WHO IS GOD TO YOU? (A)

DAY 2: WHO IS GOD TO YOU? (A)

By Ruth Abioye

25 Dec, 2020

Core Bible Verses: Ex 3:13-14


Moses said who do I tell them said me – God said, tell them I AM THAT I AM


God said to MOSES – tell them when they asked who sent you – I AM THAT I AM (this means that I AM ALL THAT YOU WANT ME TO BE; I AM ALL YOU NEED ME TO BE; I AM WHAT YOU NEED ME TO BE ANY DAY ANYTIME)


This simply connotes that


  • - God is a provider when you need provision,

  • - He is a supplier when you need supplies,

  • - He is your father when you need comfort, guidance, corrections, and directions,

  • - He is your Roadmap/GPS System when you lose your way,

  • - He is your prayer partner when you need a witness in prayer

  • - He is your comforter when you need comfort

  • - He is there to give you hope when you have lost hope

  • - He called you by His name. What you are going through, or your past does not change the name God gave to you

  • - He called you by His name. What you are going through, or your past does not change the name God gave to you

  • - He is there to give you strength and fight for you when you have lost your strength and it seems as if you are losing

  • - He is there to give you answers when you have questions, and it seems like you are losing your mind

  • - He is there to be your Light and make you a light when it seems as though darkness wants to overshadow and silence you.

GOD IS ALL YOU NEED TO HAVE ALL YOUR NEEDS MET


There is nobody in the world, neither is there any god that can be all of these things to you.


The enemy will always deceive you that you are better off without God but when you rest in THE I AM, you will realize that He has been what you have always been looking for.


MAKE IT PERSONAL

Who is God to you? Go to the scriptures and find who God is to you and how it relates to your life & current situation.


E.g. God is my help (Ps 121:1-2) – He is always there when I need Him, When I call on Him, He answers me


God is my rock (Ps 18:2)– when I am weak, He is my strength, and I can rest on Him

Now your turn… Who is God to you?
