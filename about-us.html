About Us

The Remnant Generation is a ministry that is focused on becoming a JESUS People. It is a platform through which this generation can steward their desire to learn and grow deeper in their personal relationship with God. It is a platform where you can find people of like minds and you can challenge and encourage each other to walk out their faith in everyday life. Most Christians are just satisfied with reading their bible for 5 mins and praying for the next 5 minutes and that’s all the time they get to spend with God. But God is calling us to go deeper - to find who HE IS, to find who WE ARE in Him and to walk in the anointing He has called us to.

Our Vision

To raise up a generation of youths that are focused on Jesus Christ and shine their light that men may see and glorify the Father.

Our Vision
Our Mission

To build young men and women in the order of Joshua’s and Esther’s whose hearts are focused on the Lord. To reach those who have been rejected and cast out by the help of the Holy Spirit reveal to them their worth and their value to God. To raise men and women who shall command wealth in the marketplace in the order of Solomon’s, Daniel, Joseph, and Deborah
