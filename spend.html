SPEND TIME WITH GOD

Every relationship is built on time spent.

SPEND TIME WITH GOD

DAY 5: SPEND TIME WITH GOD

By Ruth Abioye

29 Dec, 2020


Every relationship is built on time spent. A relationship that does not value time cannot last! You cannot keep writing prayer requests and new year resolutions saying you want a deeper relationship with God and not create time for that relationship to grow.


God is very interested and Jealous over the relationship He has with you – this is one of the reasons He designed the secret place – you can call it your special place or your hangout spot – where it is only you and God and nothing else matters at that point but Him.


God loves to be loved and when we decide to love Him by spending time with Him, He is pleased. God called David a man after His own heart because David always created time out of His busy schedule to spend time with God. Ps 119:164.


Your spending time with God should not be based on what you can get from Him, it should be solely because you want to know Him. Matt 6:33; Jere 29:13


MAKE IT PERSONAL

Tools you need: Journal, the bible, worship music, library/study (a quiet place)


Get your journal ready, play some worship music (not too loud); just focus on the Father. Cleanse your thoughts and consciously let your mind focus on God.


  • - You can imagine what He looks like, what Heaven is about, the sounds of angels, etc

  • - You can say God I invite you into this place to enjoy your presence and just spend quality time with you

  • - From studying the word begin to journal things you know God likes and things He doesn’t like
